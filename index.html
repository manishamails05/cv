Know Me Better

Experience

 I am passionate about coaching kids and see them blossom. Even before I became an independent educator; I spent 7 years in top notch schools and colleges as a computer teacher.
My methodology is to create interactions & continuous engagements with kids through quizzes, assignments and hands on projects.

Education

 I have done Master of Enginnering in Computer science from top ranked institute in India (UIET, Panjab Univ).
While completing my education, I also published 4 research papers in connected systems and did research in MANET to help establish wireless connectivity between computers without a wifi network.

Philosophy

 Being digital is the new Mantra for everyone and starting early helps kids in being ready for new era. But coding needs to be taught in a gentle manner as its new to the curriculam which can be bit hard on the child.
I put great emphasis on understanding child psychology and accordinly I encourage and motivate them. After every class, child will have an achievement to show.
